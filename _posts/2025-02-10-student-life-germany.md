## Tips for Future International Students

### Before Arriving
1. **Learn basic German** - even basics help immensely
2. **Research housing early** - student accommodation fills up fast
3. **Understand the bureaucracy** - lots of paperwork, but it's manageable
4. **Budget properly** - factor in all living costs

### After Arrival  
1. **Get your documents sorted ASAP** - residence permit, bank account, etc.
2. **Join student organizations** - great way to make friends
3. **Explore student job opportunities** - financial and professional benefits
4. **Embrace the culture** - don't just stick to international student groups

## Balancing Work and Studies

Working while studying requires good time management:

- **Use university tools**: Calendar apps, task management systems
- **Communicate with employers**: Be clear about exam periods and study needs  
- **Prioritize ruthlessly**: Not everything needs to be perfect
- **Take breaks**: Burnout is real, especially when adapting to a new country
